  • Understand the derivation and simplifications of the Navier-Stokes general equation for fluid flow to Reynolds-Averaged Navier-Stokes (RANS), and to 2D Saint Venant equations and their adapted form in SRH-2D, LISEM (depth-averaged dynamic wave equation), and HEC-RAS 2D (Shallow water equation).
